# WWW.AEAH.ORG-SEXYHEROS.ORG
Our mission is to address the "Digital Double-Edge": protecting individuals from the external misuse of technology (harassment and electronic abuse) while providing a framework for recovery from internal technology dependency and addiction.
This repository serves as the central digital hub and documentation archive for Against Electronic Abuse and Harassment (AEAH.ORG) and its cultural incentive program, SEXYHEROS.ORG.

Our mission is to address the "Digital Double-Edge": protecting individuals from the external misuse of technology (harassment and electronic abuse) while providing a framework for recovery from internal technology dependency and addiction.

Core Frameworks
1. AEAH (The Technical & Legal Shield)
Legislative Advocacy: Documentation of efforts to modernize the "legal books" to recognize electronic frequency abuse, neuro-technology privacy, and advanced digital stalking.

Medical Research: A repository of findings regarding "New-Era Symptoms" to help medical and mental health professionals identify physiological changes caused by technological "test-subject" environments.

Cybersecurity Defense: Open-source resources and guides for individuals to protect their digital and physical autonomy from unauthorized electronic interference.

2. SEXYHEROS (The Cultural & Awareness Front)
Strategic Partnership: A unique all-women’s initiative partnered with high-influence production entities to reach global audiences.

The "Reclaiming Goodness" Program: A rehabilitative and preventive incentive that uses influence to promote digital consent, safety, and the "Healthy Middle Ground" of device usage.

Stigma Reduction: Leveraging bold, heroic branding to make complex conversations about technology abuse accessible to everyone.

Organizational Philosophy
"Technology has advanced far past our laws and our bodies. We are currently the test subjects of a new digital era. This repository documents our path from being passive subjects to informed, protected, and empowered human beings."

Key Directories
/legal-advocacy: Drafts of proposed digital safety laws and policy research.

/medical-literacy: Resources for health professionals on technology-induced anatomy changes.

/cyber-safety: Tools and protocols for mitigating electronic harassment.

/sexyheros-outreach: Media assets and program documentation for the reclaiming goodness initiative.
