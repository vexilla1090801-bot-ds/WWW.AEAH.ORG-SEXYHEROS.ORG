The Emergency of Emergence

The United States Constitution was written for a world of one-way communication. A newspaper cannot think; a printing press cannot manipulate the neural pathways of the reader. But today, we face an "Interactive Occupant." Technology has evolved into a living organism—a conduit that does not just transmit information, but actively manipulates the human temple.

Our laws are currently "dead skin." They cling to the body of our society while the infection of technological exploitation spreads beneath. We have discovered technology, but we have not adapted to it. Until we do, we are not evolving; we are being consumed.

No system, machine, or organism shall exert two-way interactive manipulation upon a citizen without explicit, revocable, and informed consent. The temple of the human body is the final jurisdiction of the individual."
